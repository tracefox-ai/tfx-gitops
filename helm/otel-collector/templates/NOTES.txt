1. Check the OpenTelemetry Collector status:
   kubectl get opentelemetrycollector {{ include "otel-collector.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Values.namespace }}

3. View collector logs:
   kubectl logs -n {{ .Values.namespace }} -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "otel-collector.name" . }} --tail=50

4. Describe the collector resource for details:
   kubectl describe opentelemetrycollector {{ include "otel-collector.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Values.namespace }}
